Dragrift Story

Dragrift is a bold and electrifying celebration of drag culture, born in Slovakia and powered by a passion for self-expression, queerness, and community. Created as a response to the lack of queer-centered creative spaces in the region, Dragrift offers a unique stage for drag artists of all genders and styles to shine.

Each edition of Dragrift brings together performers, allies, and an enthusiastic audience for a night where boundaries are broken, identities are affirmed, and beauty comes in every form. It’s not just about wigs and heels — it’s about voice, presence, and the power of being unapologetically yourself.

From raw, emotional numbers to hilarious lip-syncs and avant-garde fashion moments, Dragrift showcases the full spectrum of drag as an art form. The event builds bridges between people, encourages freedom of expression, and fosters a supportive environment where everyone can feel seen, fierce, and fabulous.

More than just a show, Dragrift is a movement. A glittering, rebellious, high-heeled movement – glowing with pride and ready to slay.

Behind every fierce performance and flawless reveal, there’s a crew of passionate misfits making the magic happen. The Dragrift team is a chosen family of creatives, producers, artists, and dreamers who believe in the power of drag to inspire, provoke, and unite.

What started as a wild idea over coffee and contour kits has grown into one of the boldest drag platforms in the region. The team brings together diverse skills – from event production and stage design to marketing, tech, and pure queer vision. We plan, promote, rehearse, and hot glue until the stage is ready to slay.

Driven by love, fueled by glitter (and maybe a little caffeine), this crew works behind the scenes to make sure every performer feels like royalty and every guest leaves feeling empowered. We're not here for perfection – we’re here for presence, passion, and pure drag power.

No egos, no gatekeeping – just a whole lot of heart, hustle, and hairspray.
